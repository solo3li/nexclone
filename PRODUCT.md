# Product

## Register

product

## Users
General users (Everyone). Anyone looking to leverage AI tools for productivity, content creation, or daily tasks.


## Product Purpose
A centralized dashboard platform providing access to AI tools (like voice-to-text and text-to-voice) to simplify and automate workflows.

## Brand Personality
Professional, clean, reliable.

## Anti-references
Cluttered interfaces, amateurish layouts, overly complex technical dashboards.

## Design Principles
1. Practice what you preach - AI tools should feel advanced yet effortless.
2. Content over chrome - The tools and data should take center stage.
3. Expert confidence - The interface should instill trust and reliability.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Full RTL (Right-to-Left) Arabic support. High contrast for readability.
